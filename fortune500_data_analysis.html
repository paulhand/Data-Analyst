US Fortune500 2025

In this project, we grab the raw dataset via Web Scraping from 50pros.com website. Web Scraping is done by manually inspect the respective web page and copy-paste it into a text file. Then, I put it in my local webserver and successfully grab the table using Pandas module of Python directly into usable Dataframe. Why do I have to do it the hard way? Because if I directly grab it with Pandas or BeautifulSoup (maybe this one is a little out of date üòÅ), I just get 100 of them. API pulling access is not always easy to get. So, here we are, with a little tweak here and there, voila! Finally, we got the raw dataset! Let's dig it!

Out of 10 Columns/Fields and 500 rows of raw data, I try to dig deep into various insights and, turns out, many many insights I can come up with. Here's the complete list of insights I got. But, hey, wait... Let's take a brief look at how 500 biggest revenue companies spread out all over USA! Here we are!
