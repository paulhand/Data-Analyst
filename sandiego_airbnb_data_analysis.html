San Diego AirBnB 2025

In this project, we grab the raw dataset from the insideairbnb.com with usual downloading. After done with Data Cleanup, we're digging data related to AirBnB, such as how many unique properties/listings are there in San Diego per June 2025, how many unique hosts/owner currently leasing their properties, how many unique room types, the owner who has the most listings, who are the Super Host level owners, and so on.
